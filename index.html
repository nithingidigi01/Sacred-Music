Traditional Auspicious Music in Hindu Civilization

From the earliest times, Hindu civilization has regarded sound and music as sacred forces that harmonize human life with cosmic order (ṛta).

The Sāmaveda is entirely dedicated to musical chanting, showing that sacred knowledge itself was meant to be sung.

Gṛhya Sūtras prescribe music during household rites such as birth, naming, education, marriage, and funerals.

Smṛtis, Purāṇas, and the Nāṭya Śāstra affirm music as essential to temples, festivals, and social harmony.
